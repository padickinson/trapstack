TrapStack

A collection of dumb ideas involving spring-loaded traps.

TrapStack started at a bachelor party when we stacked loaded mousetraps on the floor to see how high we could go.

It was chaotic. People flinched. Traps snapped. Nobody got hurt. Two layers won.

This site documents the original game (Trap Tower) and variations people have tried since.

This is not advice. Mousetraps are mousetraps. If you do dumb things, do them responsibly.
