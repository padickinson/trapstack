TrapStack

A collection of dumb ideas involving spring-loaded traps.

TrapStack started at a bachelor party when we stacked loaded mousetraps on the floor to see how high we could go.

It was chaotic. People flinched. Traps snapped. Nobody got hurt. Two layers won.

This site documents that original game — Trap Tower — along with variations and bad ideas people have tried since.

The first game

Trap Tower is exactly what it sounds like: build the highest freestanding tower you can out of loaded mousetraps. No base. No glue. No tools. Just nerve.

A full write-up of how we played it (and what surprised us) is coming soon.
