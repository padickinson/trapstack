Welcome to TrapStack

Mouse traps aren't toys, but they're fun to play with.

TrapStack started at a bachelor party when we stacked loaded mousetraps on the floor to see how high we could go. Traps flew. Fingers were snapped. A new classic game was born.

Check out the games below, then share your triumphs and tragedies.
