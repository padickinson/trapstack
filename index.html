Welcome to TrapStack

Mouse traps aren't toys, but they're fun to play with!

TrapStack started at a bachelor party when we stacked loaded mousetraps on the floor to see how high we could go. Traps flew! Fingers were snapped! Grown men squealed like little girls! A new classic game was born.

Check out the games below, and share your triumphs and tragedies.

Trap Tower

Build the tallest freestanding stack you can using only loaded mousetraps.

Trap Pile

Players take turns placing one loaded trap on a plate until someone sets it off.
