Submit

Share your triumphs and tragedies!

If you tried Trap Tower (or invented a variant), I’d love to see it. Send whatever you have:

  • Photos of the setup or the aftermath
  • A short write-up of how you played
  • Any constraints that made it better (or worse)
  • What you’d do differently next time
Email: hello@trapstack.fun
If you want your submission credited, include the name you’d like displayed.

What makes a great submission:

  • Where and when you played
  • Number of traps and any constraints
  • The best moment (or worst collapse)

Please don’t send anything that includes people’s faces unless they’re okay being on the site. If you’re not sure, crop it or describe it.
