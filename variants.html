Variants

No official variants yet beyond Trap Tower and Trap Pile. If you invent one, we want it.

A variant is any rule tweak that makes the game feel different: team size, time limits, extra constraints, or a totally new surface to build on.

  • Smaller plate, fewer traps, faster rounds
  • Blindfolded placements (with a spotter)
  • Only one hand, or only standing pieces
